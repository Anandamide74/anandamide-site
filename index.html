Who We Are
The Science of Performance,
Inside and Out

Anandamide Sports Solutions LLP is a DPIIT-registered startup at the intersection of modern sports science and India's ancient knowledge systems. We believe human performance cannot be optimised from the outside alone — the regulatory condition of the conscious practitioner is as fundamental as their biomechanics.

The name Anandamide is drawn from the body's own endocannabinoid molecule — associated with happiness, balance, and wellbeing. Just as this molecule regulates mood and motivation from within, our programmes align physical training with emotional stability, intellectual clarity, and inner development.

Our four integrated offerings — Sports Specific Training, FitTek technology, the CBHPP research framework, and the Performance Through Wellbeing university course — form a complete ecosystem for human development at every level.

Consciousness Research
The CBHPP Framework

The dominant neuroscientific framework, while extraordinary in its proper domain, has systematically excluded the most important variable in human performance: the conscious observer. CBHPP proposes the investigation of the domain neuroscience has not yet incorporated.

Athato Brahma Jijnasa
"Now, therefore, the inquiry into the nature of consciousness."
— Brahma Sutras of Badarayana (c.500 BCE)

CBHPP draws on the unbroken lineage of first-person investigators — the Vedic Rishis, Gautama Buddha, Swami Vivekananda, Jesus Christ, Kabir, St. Francis of Assisi — whose findings represent the most sustained empirical investigation into consciousness available.

The framework introduces the critical distinction between surviving — operating from the brain's evolutionary survival apparatus — and living — what becomes possible when the mind is brought under conscious direction through yoga, meditation, and self-enquiry.

Regulatory Capacity Model
Reduced Reactivity → Regulatory Capacity → Surviving → Living → Wellbeing
Five-Level Methodology

Breath regulation, pranayama — the bridge between brain and mind

4

Consciousness Development
